Custom : 'Review, Sign & Accept' Quotation

Custom 'Review, Sign & Accept' Quotation allows you to choose the text displayed in the customer order portal view instead of 'sign & accept' and in the mail view instead of 'review, accept & sign'. With this you won't be able to scare your customers anymore.
